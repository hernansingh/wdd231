Purpose

Produday is a personal productivity and wellness project that helps you organize your day, track goals, and improve key health habits.

  • Daily planning: write goals and tasks with reminders and progress tracking.
  • Holistic wellness: log mood, sleep duration, and sleep/wake times (with a future option to sync from a smartwatch).
  • Dynamic motivation: inspirational quotes fetched via JSON/API and data-driven, actionable tips.
  • Habits visualization: simple stats and summaries using localStorage, built with accessible, responsive design and semantic HTML, optimized CSS, and JavaScript.

Target Audience Scenarios

  1. How can I stay focused on my weekly goals?

    As a student/worker with many responsibilities, I want a clear view of my priorities and timely reminders that keep me on track.

  2. Am I sleeping enough to perform better?

    As a wellness-minded user, I want to log my sleep hours and schedule to see patterns and receive suggestions to improve rest.

  3. Which habit should I improve first?

    I want simple trends/streaks that highlight my weakest habit and how it correlates with my mood.
